Overview
========

The Keyforge Clan is a medium sized family of dwarves. They are known throughout dwarf-populated regions for their security practices:

  - Master-crafted locks
  - Treasure vaults
  - Merchant/royalty guards

Background
==========

Based on providing security products and services, the Keyforge Clan has been helping merchants secure their valuables in storage and transit for three centuries.

The clan does not have their own stronghold or fortress. They are widely respected and known among many large Dwarf territories and settlements.

The clan itself is neutral. They will provide goods and services to anyone, but not to Dwarven enemies, of course.

Connections and Associations
============================

Well connected with many merchants, especially merchant and craft guilds near Dwarven territories.
